Welcome to Court Case Schedule Viewer x.y.z.
Our application will let you vie cases that are scheduled to go up for hearing on a particular day, and apply various filters for selective viewing.


Switch over to the schedule tab to view cases for a particular day by a particular court.


The Preference tab allows you to select a particular court, and set a date preference for which cases will be shown.
